Consultation Process

When you book a consultation with Corsair Code Solutions, here’s our industry-standard approach to ensure your project’s success:

  1. Discovery & Requirements Gathering
    We hold a kickoff meeting to understand your business goals, user needs, technical constraints, and success metrics.
  2. Proposal & Project Planning
    We translate requirements into a detailed scope, timeline, and budget estimate—complete with milestones and deliverables.
  3. Design & Architecture
    Our team drafts wireframes, UI mockups, and system architecture diagrams for your review and feedback.
  4. Agile Development & Iteration
    We build your solution in short sprints, delivering working software frequently and incorporating your feedback at each stage.
  5. Testing & Quality Assurance
    Comprehensive unit, integration, and user acceptance testing to ensure functionality, performance, and security.
  6. Deployment & Training
    We deploy to your environment (on-prem or cloud), configure CI/CD pipelines, and provide training for your team.
  7. Ongoing Support & Maintenance
    Post-launch monitoring, bug fixes, and enhancements to keep your software running smoothly.

Throughout the process, you’ll have a dedicated project manager, regular status updates, and transparent communication to keep everything on track and aligned with your vision.
